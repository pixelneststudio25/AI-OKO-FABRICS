Fabric Care Instructions

Proper care ensures your fabrics maintain their beauty and longevity. Follow these guidelines for different fabric types.

Chingavy Care

Hand wash in cold water with mild detergent. Avoid wringing and dry flat. Iron on low heat.

Lace Care

Dry clean recommended. If hand washing, use gentle cycle and lay flat to dry to maintain shape.

Ankara Care

Machine wash in cold water with similar colors. Tumble dry low or line dry. Iron on medium heat.

Aso Oke Care

Dry clean only. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent fading.
